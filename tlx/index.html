TLX

TLX is a Competitive Programming platform developed by enthusiast competitive programmers from Indonesia. This platform belongs to TOKI, a community of top competitive programmers in Indonesia. TLX serves as a training platform for students across Indonesia who are interested in Competitive Programming, and those who want to join the Indonesia's National Olympiad in Informatics (OSN Informatika).
